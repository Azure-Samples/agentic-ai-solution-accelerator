# Partner Onboarding Checklist

> **Audience:** partner delivery lead preparing a partner org to deliver on the accelerator.
> **Companion:** `partner-certification.md`, `competency-prerequisites.md`, `self-assessment.md`.

One-time per partner org. After completion, partner org may deliver Path A immediately; Path B requires per-engagement qualification.

---

## Organizational readiness

- [ ] Partner org has Azure AI Engineer + AI-900 certified engineers available (minimum 2).
- [ ] Partner org has at least 1 engineer with Azure Solutions Architect Expert cert.
- [ ] Partner delivery lead identified + MSFT-nominated.
- [ ] Partner org agrees to CLA on accelerator contributions.
- [ ] Partner org has a GitHub EMU or GHEC org to host customer engagements.

## Technical readiness

- [ ] Partner engineers have completed the hands-on lab (`docs/enablement/hands-on-lab.md`).
- [ ] Partner engineers have read + acknowledged `docs/supported-customization-boundary.md`.
- [ ] Partner engineers have read + acknowledged `.github/copilot-instructions.md`.
- [ ] Partner has VS Code + GitHub Copilot (Business or Enterprise) seats for engagement engineers.
- [ ] Partner has Foundry access (Azure AI Foundry project) for sandbox isolation.
- [ ] Partner has deployed and torn down a `sandbox-only` bundle in their own tenant.

## Supportability readiness

- [ ] Partner delivery lead briefed on `SUPPORT.md` intake workflow.
- [ ] Partner delivery lead briefed on `docs/governance/waiver-severity-rubric.md`.
- [ ] Partner-lead GitHub numeric actor IDs provided to MSFT for CODEOWNERS templates.
- [ ] Partner org subscribed to release manifest feed (`compatibility/releases/`).
- [ ] Partner on-call rotation defined; documented in partner delivery playbook.

## Commercial readiness

- [ ] MSFT + partner commercial alignment: scope of "MSFT-supported" vs "partner-supported" wording.
- [ ] Partner agrees no-sublicense, no-resale of the accelerator itself.
- [ ] Partner agrees to report engagement telemetry (Azure consumption proxy + attestation count) on quarterly cadence.

## Enablement milestones

- [ ] Partner completes first Path A engagement (self-guided or co-delivered).
- [ ] Partner completes first co-delivered Path B engagement with MSFT field support.
- [ ] Partner completes first solo Path B engagement (becomes certified; earns Path A "hours" claim).
