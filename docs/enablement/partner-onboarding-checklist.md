# Partner Onboarding Checklist

> **Audience:** partner delivery lead preparing a partner org to deliver on the accelerator.
> **Companion:** `self-assessment.md`.

One-time per partner org. After completion, partner org can start customer engagements using the accelerator patterns + tooling.

---

## Organizational readiness

- [ ] Partner org has Azure AI Engineer certified engineers available (minimum 2).
- [ ] Partner org has at least 1 engineer with Azure Solutions Architect Expert cert.
- [ ] Partner delivery lead identified.
- [ ] Partner org signs the CLA for any contributions back to the accelerator.
- [ ] Partner org has a GitHub org to host customer engagement repos.

## Technical readiness

- [ ] Partner engineers have walked through one reference scenario end-to-end.
- [ ] Partner engineers have read + acknowledged `docs/customization-guide.md`.
- [ ] Partner engineers have read `.github/copilot-instructions.md`.
- [ ] Partner has VS Code + GitHub Copilot (Business or Enterprise) seats for engagement engineers.
- [ ] Partner has Azure AI Foundry access in at least one subscription.
- [ ] Partner has stood up and torn down a `sandbox-only` deployment in their own tenant.

## Delivery readiness

- [ ] Partner delivery lead briefed on `SUPPORT.md` (community best-effort; partner owns the pager).
- [ ] Partner on-call rotation defined; documented in partner delivery playbook.
- [ ] Partner has an internal process for customer security review + data classification sign-off.
- [ ] Partner org subscribed to the baseline release feed.

## Commercial readiness

- [ ] Partner + Microsoft aligned on commercial relationship around the accelerator.
- [ ] Partner agrees no-sublicense, no-resale of the accelerator itself.
- [ ] Partner opts in to share engagement telemetry (Azure consumption proxy + scenario counts) on quarterly cadence.

## Enablement milestones

- [ ] Partner completes first sandbox engagement (self-guided or co-delivered).
- [ ] Partner completes first co-delivered production engagement with Microsoft field guidance.
- [ ] Partner completes first solo production engagement.
